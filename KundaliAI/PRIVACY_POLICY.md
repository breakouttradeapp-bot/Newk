# Privacy Policy – Kundali AI

**Last Updated: March 2026**

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to **Kundali AI** ("App"), an AI-powered Vedic horoscope application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our App.

By using Kundali AI, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 User-Provided Data
To generate your Kundali, we collect:
- **Full Name** – Used solely to personalize your Kundali report
- **Date of Birth** – Used for Vedic astrology calculations
- **Time of Birth** – Used for Lagna (Ascendant) and planet position calculations
- **Place of Birth** – Used to determine geographic coordinates for astrological accuracy

### 2.2 Data We Do NOT Collect
We do NOT collect:
- Phone contacts
- SMS or call logs
- Photos, videos, or files
- Device location (GPS)
- Financial information
- Passwords or authentication credentials
- Device identifiers beyond what AdMob requires

---

## 3. How We Use Your Data

Your birth data is used exclusively to:
1. Generate your personalized Kundali chart
2. Obtain AI-generated predictions via the Cerebras AI API
3. Create a downloadable PDF report (if purchased)

**We do NOT store your birth data permanently** on our servers. Data is processed in real-time and discarded after PDF generation.

---

## 4. Third-Party Services

### 4.1 Cerebras AI
We use the Cerebras AI API to generate astrological predictions. Your birth information is transmitted to Cerebras servers securely via HTTPS. Please review [Cerebras AI's Privacy Policy](https://cerebras.ai/privacy) for their data handling practices.

### 4.2 Google AdMob
We display advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect:
- Advertising ID
- Device type and OS version
- General location (country level)
- App usage data for ad targeting

See [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) for details.

### 4.3 Google Play Billing
In-app purchases (PDF unlock) are processed entirely by Google Play. We do not collect or store your payment information. See [Google Play's Terms of Service](https://play.google.com/about/play-terms/) for details.

---

## 5. Data Security

- All API communications use **HTTPS/TLS encryption**
- We do not maintain user accounts or login systems
- Birth data is not stored in any database after the session ends
- PDF files are generated locally on your device

---

## 6. Data Retention

- **Session data** (name, DOB, TOB, POB): Retained only during active app session
- **Purchase records**: Maintained by Google Play (not by us)
- **Ad data**: Managed by Google AdMob per their retention policies

---

## 7. Children's Privacy

Kundali AI is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

---
